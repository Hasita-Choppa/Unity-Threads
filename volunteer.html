The founding members of our organization offer long-term deals so that others can purchase. However, we also seek out volunteers to help us reach our goals. Volunteers can donate supplies, volunteer their time, or create products. The volunteer made products are not permanent and are very exclusive with limited stock. We will update our website as volunteers donate material for us to sell. All of the money from the sale of the volunteer-made products will go fully to charity, not just the profits. Depending on the product made, volunteers will get hours from us. If you are looking for volunteer hours or you wish to help us, consider joining our organization as either a temporary or long-term member.
